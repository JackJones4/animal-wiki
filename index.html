Elk

Here are 3 fun facts about elk!

  • Elk have a lifespan of between 10 and 13 years in the wild.
  • A male elks antlers can weigh up to 40 pounds.
  • Elks can weigh up to 700 pounds as adults.

Bison

Here are 3 fun facts about Bison!

  • Bison are the largest mammal in North America.
  • Bison calves weigh between 30 adn 70 pounds when born.
  • Male bison can reach up to 6 feet tall.

Brown Bear

Here are 3 fun facts about Brown Bears!

  • Despite being very large, brown bears can cover short distances at speeds of about 30 mph.
  • Brown bears can weigh anywhere between 300 and 1200 pounds.
  • Brown bears are most active at dusk and night.
